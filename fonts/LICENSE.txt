DejaVu Fonts (DejaVuSans, DejaVuSans-Bold, DejaVuSansMono, DejaVuSansMono-Bold)

These fonts are bundled so the PNL card renders identically regardless of
what fonts are installed on the deployment host (e.g. Render).

DejaVu fonts are a derivative of the Bitstream Vera fonts and are released
under a free, permissive license that allows redistribution, with or
without modification. Full license text:
https://dejavu-fonts.github.io/License.html
